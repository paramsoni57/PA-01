Digital Motor Starter

Microcontroller-Based Smart Motor Protection

Param Automation Digital Motor Starter continuously monitors motor voltage, current and operating conditions in real time to protect your motor, save electricity and simplify water management.

Introduction

Traditional motor starters are designed with fixed operating parameters, regardless of motor load, power conditions or application requirements. In homes and agricultural fields, this can lead to motor burnout, starter damage, excessive electricity consumption, dry running and costly maintenance.

Fluctuating voltage, overload conditions, phase failures and poor power supply can reduce motor life. Conventional starters are unable to adapt to changing conditions, leaving motors vulnerable to damage and downtime.

Param Automation has developed an advanced Microcontroller-Based Digital Motor Starter that is customizable according to the motor rated current and site requirement, ensuring maximum protection, efficiency and reliability.

Smart Protection. Smart Water Management. Smart Living.

Protect your motor from damage, save electricity, prevent water wastage and get complete peace of mind through intelligent automation.

High & Low Voltage Protection

The starter monitors supply voltage and switches off the motor during unsafe high or low voltage conditions.

Electronic Overload Protection

Advanced overload monitoring prevents excessive current draw, overheating and winding damage.

Dry Run Protection

When water is unavailable or load is insufficient, the system stops the motor to prevent burnout.

Single Phasing Protection

The starter monitors all supply phases and prevents operation if any phase is missing or faulty.

Automatic Restart Function

After temporary faults are resolved, the starter can automatically restore operation for convenience.

Auto & Manual Operation

Users can switch between automatic and manual operation whenever site conditions require it.

Fully Customizable Settings

Every protection parameter can be adjusted according to motor and site requirements.

  • Motor Current Limits
  • High Voltage Cut-Off
  • Low Voltage Cut-Off
  • Overload Settings
  • Dry Run Sensitivity
  • Restart Delay Time
  • Operating Timers
  • Water Management Functions

Built-In Automation Features

The starter is designed for reliable home and agricultural automation.

  • Timer-Based Operation for scheduled motor use
  • Long-Range Remote Operation up to 2 kilometers with compatible systems
  • Automatic Tank Filling when overhead tank level is low
  • Automatic Motor Stop when the tank is full
  • Overflow Prevention for efficient water usage
